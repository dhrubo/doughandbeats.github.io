Our Story

Dough & Beats is an independent mobile food business based in London, founded by long-time friends Nits and Dhru. We are committed to quality, creativity, and good vibes.

Our Food

Our dough is made daily, fermented for flavour, and cooked until crisp on the outside and airy on the inside. Each sandwich is filled with high-quality ingredients including Italian meats, fresh vegetables, and artisan cheeses.

Our Vibe

We bring the energy of vibrant street food culture to private events, festivals, and local markets across London. We’ll bring the fire — in both food and music.
