DOUGH & BEATS

Steeetside Panouzzo. Rhythm & Soul.

Dough & Beats is an independent mobile food business based in London, serving up handcrafted, contemporary Neapolitan-style pizzas with a twist — pizza sandwiches baked fresh in our portable ovens.

Our dough is made daily, fermented for flavour, and cooked until crisp on the outside and airy on the inside. Each sandwich is filled with high-quality ingredients including Italian meats, fresh vegetables, and artisan cheeses — always bold, always flavourful.

We bring the energy of vibrant street food culture to private events, festivals, and local markets across London.

Founded by long-time friends Nits and Dhru, Dough & Beats is committed to quality, creativity, and good vibes. Whether you're planning a wedding, a company party, or a community fair, we’ll bring the fire — in both food and music.

Fully insured. Registered food business. Based in London.
